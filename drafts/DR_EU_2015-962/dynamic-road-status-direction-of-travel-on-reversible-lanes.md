**Dynamic road status – _direction of travel on reversible lanes_:**
dynamic information disseminating the active direction of travel on a reversible lane of a specific segment of a road link (or on the entire road link).
>The unambiguous description of direction of travel on reversible lanes requires the provision of information about its time validity.
