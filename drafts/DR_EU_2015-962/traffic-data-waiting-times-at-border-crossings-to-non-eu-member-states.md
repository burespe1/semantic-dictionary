**Traffic data – _waiting times at border crossings to non-EU Member States_:**
dynaming information indicating the average time required for vehicles to wait between their arrival at the queue of a border crossing (if any) and their departure.
>The scope of this data category is limited to border crossings from EU Member States to non-EU Member States.
