**Other static and dynamic traffic regulations:**
the minimum information required for disseminating and describing the content of other traffic rules and driving regulations that are either permanent (static) or changeable over time (dynamic), considering ongoing events and prevailing conditions.

>The current data category follows a similar structure to “Static and dynamic traffic regulations”. It is associated with other machine-interpretable rules and restrictions that may, for instance, relate to parking, pedestrian zones, HOV lanes, bicycle lanes, and residential areas.
